Privacy Policy – Nextbite

Last updated: 23 Nov 2025

Nextbite (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is a fasting timer and history tracker. We respect your privacy and keep data handling minimal.

Information We Collect

We do not create accounts or upload personal data to our servers. All fasting entries and settings stay on your device’s local storage. Removing the app deletes this information.

Ads & Analytics

The App shows banner ads via Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and usage data to deliver and measure ads. You can review Google’s policies at policies.google.com/privacy. We request consent where required and honor non-personalized ads if consent is declined.

App Permissions

The App only needs basic network access to load ads and uses local storage for your fasting history. We do not request optional permissions like camera or contacts.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information. AdMob receives ad request data solely to serve ads in the App.

Security

All data remains on your device. We rely on the security provided by your operating system and Google Play for distribution.

Your Choices

You may reset advertising consent through Android’s ads settings or by reinstalling the App. To delete local data, uninstall the App—there is no server data to erase.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, email us at support@nextbite.app.
